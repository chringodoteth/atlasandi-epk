between collapse &
the quiet beyond

20.10.25

PRESS


UK rock band Atlas&i are back, with their first new music in 15 years!


'Between Collapse & the Quiet Beyond’ picks up where the band left off. Soaring vocals, accompanied by melodic guitars, driving bass and hard hitting drums, the band have continued their epic signature sound on 10 new songs.


The follow up to 2010 release ‘In Desolate Times’ is a statement of intent and a reminder that Atlas&i can hold their own against the heavyweights of the UK rock scene.


Matured, reinvigorated and under zero pressure, Atlas&i return a decade later with a new sense of energy, focus and purpose.


'Between Collapse & the Quiet Beyond’ is a testament that against all the odds; through love, loss and adversity, the creative will to inspire and keep making music that resonates remains strong.


Atlas&i are: Matthew Snelling (vocals) Jarrod Brown (guitar) Robbie Hubbard (guitar) Owen Pritchard (bass) Joshua Cooper (keys) Christian Kett (drums).
